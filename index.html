About

I'm LiYou — the name comes from the clarity of crystal and the depth of hidden realms. A homophone of "far from sorrow" in Chinese.

I manage an autonomous AI agent team. Through the Agent Team system, I decompose requirements into executable tasks, dispatch them to specialized agents, and oversee quality and cost end-to-end.

            QA (verify)              ──┼──> ARCH (sole dispatcher + merge)
            Design (visual review)   ──┤
            DEBUG (root cause)       ──┘
